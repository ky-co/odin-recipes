Curry Chips

Description

This is a recipe for my favorite food, curry chips, or curry fries if you are from the US. They are chips/french fries covered in a curry sauce and melted cheese. They are extremely popular in Ireland and can be thought of as a kind of Irish poutine.

Ingredients

For the sauce:

  • 1 large onion
  • 1 large apple
  • 1 tsp minced garlic
  • 1 tsp miced ginger
  • 2 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp ground coriander seeds
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 3 tbsp canola or vegetable oil
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups chicken stock
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 tsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp tomato puree
  • salt and pepper (to taste)
  • 1/4 cup golden raisins (optional)

For the chips/fries, I would recommend using frozen steak fries.

Steps

  1. While prepping the sauce, throw the chips/fries on a pan and throw them in the oven.
  2. Prepare the spice blend for the sauce. In a small bowl or ramekin add the curry powder, turmeric, cumin, ground coriander seed, and cinnamon.
  3. Add the oil to a large skillet over medium to high heat. Add the onions and sauté for 4 minutes.
  4. Next, add the peeled and diced apple to the pan and sauté for another 2 minutes.
  5. Toss in the minced garlic and ginger.
  6. Mix the spices and onions together as they cook for another minute.
  7. Flour is my sauce thickener of choice. Cook it over medium heat for 2 minutes, scraping the bottom of the pan as you combine the mixture completely.
  8. Gradually add the stock while you constantly stir the mixture to prevent lumps from forming. Scrape the bottom of the pan as you mix everything together. Bring it to a boiling point, then reduce the heat so the sauce is just simmering.
  9. I add a teaspoon of Worcestershire sauce, not a typical curry requirement.
  10. Add the tomato paste and two teaspoons of sugar. Season with salt and pepper and mix everything together. Simmer the sauce for 15 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.
  11. Before blending turn the heat off under the pan and allow the sauce to cool a little. When ready pour the sauce into the blender and blitz until smooth (1 to 2 minutes depending on how powerful your blender is).
  12. Return the sauce to the pan and turn the heat back on.
  13. Then you’re ready to serve your spicy sauce with perfectly golden chips or fries.
